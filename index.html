Do People in Bedford Know Where to Access Support?

What We Found:

2 out of 3 residents didn’t know where their nearest walk-in health clinic is.
Most people reported they don’t have many areas to access support in Bedford.
Over half didn’t know about local organisations such as
Recovery Lounge Bedford, King’s Arm Project, and Job Centre Plus.

Why This Matters

We thought—what’s the point in having support organisations if people don’t even know they exist?

Instead of adding another organisation to the noise, we’ve built a simple and effective way to raise awareness of what’s already available — from mental wellbeing to employment support, health services, and homelessness support.

Our solution: A Map!

Scan to view our colour-coded map of Bedford highlighting help centres, wellbeing hubs, youth development areas, outdoor wellbeing spaces and local services — plus phone numbers!
